About Me

I am a self-taught Web Developer based in Manchester, with a strong interest in UI/UX design. I am looking to break into the industry and develop my skills.

My current knowledge favours frontend development, encompassing - but not limited to:

  • a strong foundation in HTML, CSS (including BEM methodology) and JavaScript
  • UI & State Management Libraries: React & Redux (including Redux Sagas)
  • SASS & CSS-in-JS (specifically, Styled Components)

I have a growing knowledge of the backend and backend platforms, including some experience with:

  • Firebase and Firestore (a NoSQL Realtime Database)
  • Node.js and SQL

I earned a Bachelors of Science in IT at Sheffield Hallam University, where I was first introduced to fundamental concepts in programming & software development.

I don't skimp on theory BUT I think programming is best learned through practically applying concepts. Most of what I know comes from many hours of my own research & experimentation, as well as online project-based courses & tutorials. Ultimately, I believe this hands-on approach has helped me to adopt a mindset for growth and gives me confidence in my abilities as a developer.

The core of my passion for Web Development lies in the freedom it provides to exercise creativity alongside analytical thinking, two things that aren't often synonymous in other professions. For me, it's this juxtaposition and multifaceted nature of web development that maintains my interest - there is never a dull moment and always something to learn.

Discord is a popular online social platform with many programming communities. Throughout my learning experience, I have often turned to such channels when in need of help. When possible, I like to give back by working with others to solve their problems. This has taught me the value of teamwork, and is why I look forward to working with a team and like-minded people in general.

I am currently job-seeking - get in touch. My immediate goal is to apply & level-up my frontend skills in real-world projects (whether it be JS, React or CSS). Determining how users will interact with a website/web app has spiked my interest in UI/UX design, so I'd like to continue exploring this as a developer.

When the opportunity presents itself - either in the workplace or in my own time - I aim to:

  • strengthen my understanding of the backend - e.g. using Node.js / Python / PHP (or equivalent) to build APIs, security, SQL vs NoSQL etc.
  • improve understanding of Accessibility on the web
  • expand my skillset to include a statically typed language (like TypeScript or C#)

Book Worm

A MERN Stack Book Tracking application with CRUD functionality. The UI has been developed using Material UI (v4).

Features include:

  • Cross-origin requests - query and display data from the Google Books API in various ways
  • User authentication and authorization with bcrypt and JWT-Cookies, respectively
  • Registered users can:
    • create and name their own booklists
    • add books to & delete books from a booklist
    • update details of a booklist or delete the list and its contents
